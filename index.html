What is BJJ?

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial arts that focuses on leverage and ground fighting. Today, BJJ is used in a sport format and also for self-defense. Practitioners of BJJ can be found using the style to do everything from improving their overall health to training for actual combat. Many of the techniques from this grappling art can also be found in other sports such as Mixed Martial Arts and Catch Wrestling.

Wolfpack BJJ is an inclusive martial arts academy that focuses on combining the core principles of self defence and sport jiu jitsu. We pride ourselves on bringing out the best of our students. We come from different walks of life but all strive towards the same goal, getting 1% better every day.

Classes

Gi Training

Learn how to use your opponents clothing against them. Gi classes focus on acquiring grips and using them to manipulate and entangle your partner.

No-Gi Training

No-Gi training focuses more on fluid movements and understanding body mechanics since you won't have access to your opponents kimono.

Competition Class

Competition class is a slightly more intensive class compared to the Gi and No-Gi training. Competition class focuses on drilling and repition with situational sparring.

Adult Classes

Our adult classes are great for anyone starting out, from beginners to the intermediate grappler, all are welcome!

Kids Classes

Our kids classes focuses on strengthening our kids mentally, as well as physically, by incorporating grappling games to challenge our kids to use what they learned in an effective manner.

Private Lessons

Private lessons are a great way to focus on areas of improvement or to expand your game.

Physical Benefits

If you’re trying to shed the extra pounds, BJJ is a great form of exercise. You’re constantly engaging multiple muscle groups in every Jiu Jitsu class. The constant movement from warmups at the start of class to rolling during open mat will help you burn calories. Since grappling arts such as BJJ involve your entire body, your cardiovascular system will get stronger.

One of the natural benefits of learning BJJ is you improve your flexibility and overall mobility. BJJ teaches you how to move more efficiently. Your range of motion will be better as a result of doing warmups and drilling techniques you learn in class. Your body will get stronger as you train consistently.

Mental Benefits

Consistently doing difficult things will help you be able to focus more. BJJ teaches you to be mentally present. You learn how to commit your brainpower towards whatever you’re trying to accomplish such as finishing a submission or recovering from a hard throw. This ability to focus will help you in other areas of your life such as work and family relationships.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu gives you regular opportunities to test out solutions to problems you’re dealing with on the mat. You receive instant feedback as to whether or not what you’re doing works. This style of learning can be encouraging as you encounter problems outside of your gym since learning a new skill helps you develop stronger neural connections.

Other Benefits

While training can be physically and mentally challenging, it’s not uncommon to see everyone on the mat smiling. You’ll have a great time as you work with your friends and grow together on your Jiu Jitsu journey.

There’s nothing like the BJJ community! You’ll get to experience the bond that comes from training together. As you learn and grow, you will be able to create lifelong memories and friendships.
